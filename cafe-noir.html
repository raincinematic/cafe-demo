The Story

Espresso, After Dark

Café Noir began with one question: what if a coffee shop felt like the opening scene of a film? Low light. Warm amber. A room that slows you down the moment you walk in.

Our beans are roasted dark and slow in small batches. Our espresso pulls rich and heavy, crowned with crema the color of old gold. No noise, no rush, no fluorescent anything.

This is Miami's quiet side. Come find it.
